The first chart shows how each player performed in separate games against 1-3 base (Adam) opponents. The bar labeled Adam shows how well the Adam avatar played against 1-3 identical players. There are equal numbers of 2-player, 3-player, and 4-player games. The second chart shows how well all the players fared against each other. Every possible permutation was played, some multiple times to make the number of 2-player, 3-player, and 4-player games equal.

Win percentage and win share are slightly different, since a game can have more than one winner. Win percentage is the fraction of all games won by the player. Win share is the fraction of all wins claimed by that player. For a large set of games, the win percentage is generally a few percentage points higher than win share for each player.
